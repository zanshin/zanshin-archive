Mark Hanford Nichols

i was born in brooklyn, new york, and raised in decatur, illinois. my major interests as a child were swimming and taking things apart.

in high school i discovered computers. the first programs i wrote were in basic, done with optical scan cards, paper tape spools, and a tty terminal. i still have some of those early games on paper tape spools.

i studied computer science at illinois state university, focussing on the then hot topics of jcl, cics, and vsam. i have a bachelor of science degree with a minor in business administration.

after college i moved to springfield, illinois. my interests there included the local bicycle club and karate-do. i hold a nidan in shudo-kan karate-do and have competed at the national level several times.

i met my wife, michele, online via the internet. we spent a year talking on the phone and meeting half way between colorado springs and springfield. we were married on july 26, 1997. she is a psychotherapist with a private practice.

in the spring of 1998 we moved to vancouver, washington. it is one of the greenest places in this country. it is also one of the wettest. it never rains hard but it does rain almost every day. the winter time cloud cover lasts from late september until march or april.

in the early summer of 1999 we moved cross country to charleston, south carolina. we purchased a home in summerville, about 30 west of charleston. the move to charleston put us in the middle of our familes. with friends a relatives in florida, north carolina, tennessee, and illinois; south carolina was a good choice.

after losing my job in may of 2000 and regaining it in july of 2000 we decided to move again. back to central illinois. currently i am working for myself as a contractor for the state of illinois.
