The views expressed on this page are those of the author. They do NOT reflect the views, policies, or opinions of CIPS, Union Electric or Ameren Corporation.
In August 1995 CIPSCO announced a merger with Union Electric of St. Louis. Since that time many of the people in the Information Services Department have left for other opportunities. The "Dilbert" Attrition Countdown Chart was created to track where everyone went and when. One final note: Everyone is listed in the order their last date became known, not in the order they actually left.
