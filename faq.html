What is Zanshin?
Zanshin is a martial arts concept that roughly translates to remaining mind. It deals with your focus and its movement from one situation to another. For me personally it sums up my approach to life. You can read more about my take on it here.

How is this site put together and made available to the general public?
I use MoveableType as my content management software. Previous I was using Blogger but I didn't like the input interface. Prior to that I was hand coding everything.

Today I use BBEdit and CSSEdit on Mac OS x (10.2.6) to craft the pages, and Transmit to FTP them to my host, pair.com. Once there I use MoveableType to manage the content.

The current page layout for my site comes from glish.com/css. It is an excellent resource that I highly recommend.

Why have a site?
Why not?

More to the point, I find exposing certain aspects of myself through this medium to be gratifying. By writing down what I am thinking and feeling and then sharing it for the whole world to see, I grow. I learn about myself in a very real way. I also enjoy the technical challenge of making this site look and feel the way I want.

Is this site old enough to vote?
No. But it could attend the third grade. The earliest version of zanshin.net went live in February of 1995. Prior to that I had a site at my local ISP for about a year. So zanshin is 8 years old, more or less.

Who is the author?
I am Mark. I live and work in central Illinois with hopes of moving back to the Pacific Northwest someday soon. I have been in data processing for 20 years, I studied karate for 8 years, and I've been married for 6 years. I live with my wife, Michele, and our two cats, Nekko and Taz.

I notice that some of the earliest entries have high post numbers. What's up with that?
I started using MoveableType in June of 2002. Once I was comfortable with it and knew I would be continuing its use, I exported my earlier Blogger postings, and added them to my archive here. I also found the ancient hand-coded posting on my harddrive and uploaded them as well. The dates are chronologically correct.
