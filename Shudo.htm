Shudo-Kan Karate-do Club of Springfield, Illinois

W elcome to the homepage of the Shudo-Kan Karate-do Club of Springfield, Illinois. Founded in 1972 by Doug and Sandy Dennis it is the oldest karate dojo in the Springfield area. Providing instruction in Women's self-defense, Karate-do, Karate-jujitsu, and Okinawan Kobudo our club offers a wide range of martial arts.

Shudo-Kan is a member of the National Karate Association and the United States of America Karate Federation (USAKF).
