Fellow Travelers,

For the past three years my husband and I have pursued our own personal perfection. Part of that journey is seeking Agapé. We believe that this part of the journey is best when shared. Towards that end we are offering to share a weekend with you and your partner. This weekend will be an intense experience. It will scour the depths of your soul, and fill you with love to the heights of your being.

During this weekend we will explore the barriers that each of us has erected between ourselves and our goals. You will learn techniques and tools that will improve your communication with your partner, and more importantly, with yourself. This is not a quick fix, nor will you be "cured" by attending. Instead this is a course in how to fish in the sea of personal relationships and goals.

Any journey beings with but a single step, we feel that this is a very good first step. Please contact us for more information.

In truth,
Michele and Mark Nichols
