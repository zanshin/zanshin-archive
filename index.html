9.11.01

I have been on a journey inward for some time now. The stories, observations, and writings here observe the mileposts and waystations of this journey.

This latest incarnation of zanshin.net comes from a need to share more, to expose more of myself. For it is only through feedback and scrutiny that we truly grow. Rare are those who can grow independently of others. I need the contact and friction that foreign thoughts and ideas cause.

It is winter. A time of slumber and deep rest. But deep inside me, seeds of change lie, hording their energy to alter my journey. I control the spring within me. I alone can release the sun and rain needed to start the new season of growth.

I invite you, dear reader, to accompany me on this leg of my journey. You cannot walk in my path, but you are welcome to observe from your vantage point. Perhaps you will learn from my travails. Perhaps you will share yours insights with me.

Watch.

Look.

Learn.

Grow.

Play.

Think.

Laugh.

Cry.

Love.
