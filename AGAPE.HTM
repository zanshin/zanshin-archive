Agapé is a term which means 'total love and acceptance. We believe that this is the ultimate goal of every essence living on this planet; to atain a state of agapé.

Most of us learn conditional love and conditional acceptance from our life experiences. This input comes at us from all sides. Our families, schools, media, friends, and indeed, ourselves.

How many times have we all heard words to this effect, "If you would only do this", "If you would only be this", "I love you, but..." Over and over we are conditioned to believe that love is supposed to have strings. If you aren't perfect then you can't be totally loved and accepted. Of course this begs the question, what is perfect.

We are all perfect. Each of us is exactly the way we are for a reason. To learn something from this life experience. Fighting what we are in order to meet the popularly held ideal of perfect is to fight one's own nature. Fighting who you real are negates the very purpose of your life here.
